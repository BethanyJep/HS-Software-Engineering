# Tech Careers Panel and Group Discussion

**Date:** Saturday, 25 July 2026  
**Speakers:** Four professionals from a mix of technology roles, such as software engineering and data analysis  
**Format:** Career-experience panel followed by a facilitated group discussion

---

## Session 1 – Career-Experience Panel

Invite each speaker to briefly introduce their role and share how they entered the technology field, what their work involves, and an experience that shaped their career.

### Panel Questions

1. What path led you to your current role, and what challenge or turning point had the greatest influence on your journey?
2. What does your role contribute to a technology team, and which skills would you encourage students to start developing now?

---

## Session 2 – Group Discussion

After the panel, divide participants into small groups. Ask each group to discuss the questions below, choose a note-taker, and share one key insight with everyone.

### Discussion Questions

1. What similarities and differences did you notice across the speakers' roles and career journeys, and which experience resonated with you most?
2. Which technology career would you like to explore further, and what is one practical step you could take to learn more about it?
